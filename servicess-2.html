AI Overview

Blood donation is a safe and relatively painless procedure that can help save lives:

Eligibility

Healthy adults between the ages of 18 and 75 can donate blood if they meet the eligibility criteria.

Procedure

During a donation, you'll give about 470 milliliters of blood, which is about 8% of the average adult's blood volume.

Recovery

Your body replaces the blood volume within 24 to 48 hours, and replenishes red blood cells in 10 to 12 weeks.

Donation process

You can:

  • Check that you're eligible to donate
  • Sign up online or call to make an appointment
  • Follow the recommendations for preparing to donate
  • Have a trained staff member review your questionnaire answers and perform a finger prick test to check your hemoglobin level

You can learn how and where to donate blood locally by: Calling 1-800-RED-CROSS and Checking out the American Red Cross online.
